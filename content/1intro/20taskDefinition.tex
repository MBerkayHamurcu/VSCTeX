\Equation{Boole's inequality}{
  \begin{equation}
    P(\bigcup_{n=1}^n A_n) \leq \sum_{n=1}^n P(A_n)
  \end{equation}
}

Where the events $A_n$ are disjoint, then the inequality
in equation \ref{Boole's inequality} becomes an equality.
\unsure{Ist das richtig?}

\Equation{Pythagorean Theorem}{
  \begin{equation}
    a^2+b^2=c^2
  \end{equation}
}

On an unrelated note, equation \ref{Pythagorean Theorem} defines the length of
the sides of a right-angle triangle, where $c$ represents the hypotenuse.\unsure{Und das auch?}
